Why the UChicago CI Resource Explorer?

It's sometimes confusing what services exist and what services they provide. This resource explorer helps you quickly narrow down where you might best be served for a particular use case. Using it, you can select:

  1. Type: Are you looking for compute? Storage? Something in the cloud? etc
  2. Service: Do you want to filter down to resources only available for specific services?
  3. Audience: the intended audience that you need (e.g., staff, students, faculty)
  4. Scale: Do you need Gigabytes, or Petabytes?
  5. Protected Data: Is your data protected or sensitive?
Once you have made one or more selections, the options on the right side of the page will filter down. You can click on any option to be taken to the documentation base for each, or contact us directly to ask a question.
